# Team Culture Guide

## Our Values in Action

### Psychological Safety

We believe everyone should feel safe to speak up, ask questions, and make mistakes. If you are afraid to ask a "dumb question," that is a signal we need to fix our culture, not a signal about you.

**How we practice it:**
- Managers share their own mistakes in team meetings
- We celebrate "learning moments" in retrospectives
- No question is too basic in #engineering-help

### Feedback Culture

We give feedback early and often. Waiting for a performance review to share important feedback is a failure of leadership.

**How to give good feedback:**
- Be specific: "In the PR review yesterday, the comment about the database query was unclear" not "your reviews are confusing"
- Be timely: Give feedback within 24-48 hours of the event
- Be kind: Assume positive intent; focus on behavior and impact, not character

### Work-Life Integration

We work hard and we rest. Sustainable pace is a feature, not a bug. If you are regularly working more than 45 hours a week, talk to your manager — something is wrong with our planning, not with you.

## Belonging and Inclusion

### Employee Resource Groups

- **Women in Tech** — Monthly meetups, mentorship program
- **LGBTQ+ Alliance** — Safe space, advocacy, events
- **BIPOC in Tech** — Community, mentorship, career development
- **Parents & Caregivers** — Flexible work support, community

### Mentorship Program

Every new employee is matched with a mentor outside their direct team for the first 90 days. Your mentor is a resource for:
- Navigating company culture
- Career advice
- Introductions to people across the company

Contact hr@acme.com to be matched if you have not been already.

## Recognition

We recognize contributions publicly and privately:
- **Shoutouts** in #kudos Slack channel — anyone can post
- **Spot bonuses** — managers can award for exceptional contributions
- **Quarterly awards** — team-nominated, company-wide recognition

Do not wait to be recognized — recognize others. A culture of appreciation starts with each of us.
